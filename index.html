ENVIRONMENT ACTION CONFERENCE & EXPO 2022

We are excited we could still come together to learn, share, strengthen and create environmental initiatives with positive impacts on the planet.
Uniting independent efforts to create powerful change in Latin America, it will bring together initiatives from 33 countries, in June, in Bolivia.

2022.03.25(FRI) ~ 28(MON)

@ Plurinational Cultural Center, La Paz

Main Program

  • Lecture

    Listen to the speakers from various countries about their successful initiatives and challenges.

  • Exibihition

    Showcase your innovative projects and appreciate those of other participants.

  • Forum

    Have the time to share your thoughts and opinions with experts and experienced activists.

  • Discussion groups

    Actively participate to strengthen the quality of your initiatives and those of the group.

  • Networks

    It will be the initial space to create networks and possible future alliances and collaborations.
