ENVIRONMENT ACTION
CONFERENCE AND EXPO 2022

Environment Action brings together the community of institutions and activists that lead environmental initiatives in different countries of Latin America.
The protection of the environment must be a transversal objective in the different areas in which the population carries out its activities, only then will it have a sustainable positive impact. For this reason, this year we want to extend the invitation to different initiatives related to one or more of the sustainable development goals to 2030 not necessarily named environmental, but which in turn has an indirect positive impact on the protection of the environment.
If you have any initiative with these objectives, we hope you will consider joining us this year to contribute to the strengthening of the protection of the planet.

Please contact us per Email for any further question about EA Latin America Conference and Expo 2022!
